# 🛒 Shopify E-Commerce Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of a Shopify E-Commerce dataset containing **60,000 customer orders** collected between **January 2023 and June 2025**.

The objective of this analysis is to uncover valuable business insights related to:

* Sales performance
* Product profitability
* Discount effectiveness
* Return behavior
* Marketing channel performance
* Geographic trends
* Customer satisfaction

The project demonstrates how data analytics can support data-driven business decisions and revenue growth strategies.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The analysis aims to answer the following key business questions:

### 📈 Sales Performance

* How has revenue and profit changed over time?
* Which months and quarters generate the highest sales?

### 📦 Product Category Analysis

* Which categories contribute the most revenue and profit?
* Which categories require improvement?

### 🏷 Discount Optimization

* How do discounts affect revenue and profitability?
* Is the current discount strategy effective?

### 🔄 Return Analysis

* Which products have the highest return rates?
* Which countries experience the most returns?

### 📣 Marketing Effectiveness

* Which traffic sources generate the most revenue and orders?
* Which channels deserve more marketing investment?

### 🌍 Geographic Insights

* Which countries generate the highest revenue?
* Which markets show the greatest growth potential?

### 📊 Correlation Analysis

* What factors influence revenue and profit?
* How strongly are business metrics related?

## 📈 Key Insights

### 💎 Electronics Leads the Business

* Highest revenue generating category.
* Highest profit margin among all categories.

### 📅 Strong Seasonality

* Q1 and Q2 outperform Q3 and Q4.
* May records the highest revenue.

### 🏷 Discounts Reduce Profitability

* Higher discounts show a negative relationship with revenue and profit.
* Quantity sold impacts revenue more than discounts.

### 🔄 High Return Rate

* Overall return rate is 14.81%.
* Sports and Fashion categories show the highest returns.

### 📣 Marketing Channels Perform Similarly

* Social Media generates the highest revenue.
* Revenue differences between channels are relatively small.

### 🌍 Geographic Opportunities

* Australia generates the highest revenue.
* India has the highest customer satisfaction score.
* UK market shows expansion potential.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### ✅ Implement Category-Based Discounts

Reduce discounts on high-performing categories and increase promotions for underperforming products.

### ✅ Reduce Product Returns

Improve product descriptions, sizing information, and customer reviews.

### ✅ Strengthen Seasonal Campaigns

Increase marketing efforts during Q3 and Q4 to combat seasonal sales declines.

### ✅ Improve Customer Satisfaction

Introduce loyalty programs and post-purchase feedback initiatives.

### ✅ Scale Social Media Marketing

Invest more in high-performing social media campaigns and influencer marketing.
